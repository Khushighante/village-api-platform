# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Unable to access workspace (shows “No workspace available”)
**Answer:** Please try clearing your browser cache or logging in using Incognito / Private mode.

### 2. Is the Capstone Project a team project or individual?
**Answer:** The Capstone Project is an individual project.

### 3. Where do I need to submit tasks or the project?
**Answer:** You do not need to submit individual tasks. Once your project is complete, you will submit the combined module/project. Submission guidelines will be shared via email.

### 4. Where can I take the Pre-Test?
**Answer:** You will receive an email with instructions.
